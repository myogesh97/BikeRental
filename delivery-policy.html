Home Delivery and Pick-up Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: August 28, 2025

By availing Batmobilz Rentals home delivery and pick-up services, the customer agrees to the following terms:

1. Delivery Charges

Delivery and pick-up services are chargeable. Charges are determined based on the minimum order value, selected time slot, and the delivery address.

2. Non-Refundable Charges

Delivery charges are non-refundable. The only exception is if the entire booking is cancelled, in which case the Batmobilz Rentals Standard Refund & Cancellation Policy will apply.

3. Delivery Protocol

  • The delivery executive will wait at the designated address for a maximum of 15 minutes. If the customer is not present, the situation will be treated as a ‘no-show,’ and no rescheduling or refund will be permitted.
  • In the event of a delivery delay exceeding 60 minutes from the scheduled time, the customer will be informed. A full refund of the delivery charges will be processed upon the customer's request.

4. Address Change & Pickup Option

  • The delivery address cannot be altered after the booking is confirmed.
  • Customers may request to switch from home delivery to a station pick-up. In such cases, delivery charges will be refunded as per the Standard Refund & Cancellation Policy.

5. Right to Amend Services

Batmobilz Rentals reserves the right to alter, suspend, or withdraw delivery services without prior notice.

6. Policy Compliance

All modifications, cancellations, and special requests are governed by the Batmobilz Rentals Standard Refund & Cancellation Policy.

7. Customer Responsibilities at Delivery

To ensure a smooth and secure handover, the customer is required to:

  • Be Present: The individual who made the booking must be present at the delivery location at the scheduled time. The vehicle will not be handed over to a third party.
  • Provide Identification: The customer must present their original, valid driver's license and a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport) for verification, as per the Rider Eligibility terms.
  • Ensure Safe Location: The customer must ensure that the delivery location is safe, accessible, and has adequate space for the delivery executive to park and conduct the vehicle handover process.

8. Vehicle Handover and Inspection

The customer's responsibility for the vehicle begins at the time of handover. Before accepting the motorcycle, the customer must:

  • Conduct Inspection: Thoroughly inspect the motorcycle for any existing damages, scratches, or mechanical issues. All observations must be noted on the rental agreement or a pre-delivery inspection form.
  • Sign Agreement: Sign the rental agreement to confirm acceptance of the motorcycle's condition. Once signed, the customer is liable for any new damages found upon return.
  • Verify Documents: Ensure that the motorcycle's documents, such as the registration certificate and insurance papers, are present with the vehicle.

9. Service Area and Operating Hours

Our home delivery and pick-up services are subject to certain limitations:

  • Service Area: This service is available only within specific geographical limits (e.g., within the municipal limits of Guwahati). Please confirm if your address falls within our service area before booking.
  • Operating Hours: Deliveries and pick-ups can be scheduled between 09:00 AM and 09:00 PM, subject to availability of time slots.

10. Pick-up Protocol

For the vehicle pick-up at the end of the rental period:

  • Vehicle Condition: The customer must return the vehicle in the same condition it was received, barring normal wear and tear.
  • Fuel Policy: The vehicle should have the same amount of fuel as was provided at the time of delivery. Charges for fuel shortages will be applicable as per the pricing policy.
  • Post-Rental Inspection: The customer must be present during the post-rental inspection conducted by the pick-up executive. Any new damages will be noted and charged from the security deposit or as per the vehicle damage policy.

11. Unforeseen Circumstances (Force Majeure)

Batmobilz Rentals shall not be held liable for any delay or failure in service due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control. This includes but is not limited to natural disasters, floods, fire, civil unrest, government restrictions, or extreme weather conditions.
