For us, it's more than just renting a motorcycle; it's about providing the key to unlock true adventure. Based in the heart of Guwahati, we are a team of passionate riders dedicated to sharing the raw beauty and thrilling roads of Northeast India with fellow explorers.

  • Regularly scheduled club rides and scenic tours

  • Technical workshops and knowledge-sharing sessions

  • Family-friendly social events and annual meets

We believe the ultimate way to connect with this land is from the saddle of a motorcycle. We invite you to create your own stories while navigating the breathtaking landscapes of the Northeast—from the lush tea gardens of Assam to the unforgettable mountain passes of Arunachal Pradesh.

  • A strong commitment to preserving motorcycle history

  • A welcoming and inclusive community for all enthusiasts

  • Promoting safe and responsible riding practices
