Charges & Penalties Policy

Batmobilz Rentals is committed to complete transparency with our riders. Below is a detailed list of charges applicable in specific scenarios after your reservation is confirmed.

Reservation Charges & Policies

Scenario Applicable Charges
Cancellation (up to 24 hrs before ride) Partial refund available. (50% of the booking amount)
Cancellation (within 24 hrs of ride) No Refund, Full booking amount is forfeited.
Extension of Booking Subject to availability. Per hour/ per day Charges applicable as per the vehicle rented.
Shortening post start No refund will be provided in this case.
Late Return Incase of without prior intimation Late return penalties entail a charge of 100 plus double the hourly rental rate of the vehicle.

Refund Policy Notes

  • All refunds shall be made through the original payment mode within 7-10 working days.
  • The refunds for the repairs & maintenance done by customers on the vehicles shall be processed within 7-10 working days. The customer is required to share the bills with the Batmobilz rentals team.

Penalties for Violations

Violation Penalty / Policy
Traffic and Parking violations Full payment of fines as applicable will be born by the renter.
No Show Bookings would stand cancelled if after 4 hours of booking start time the customer still doesn't show up. No refunds would be applicable. 9]
Key/Helmet not returned at end of reservation Replacement cost will be born as per actual. Key replacement cost on service provider estimate.
Vehicle Damage Incase of damage to the vehicle on the lease period, due to accident, mishandling, carelessness appropriate charges will be calculated as per the bill based on service centre estimate accordingly the renter shall be liable to pay the same along with the daily tariff until the vehicle is ready for renting again. Entire claim amount has to be paid by the customer upfront in advance.
Towing and Impounding No cost will be charged to customer if caused by vehicle failure & full cost to be charged to customer if caused by user negligence.
Helmet Complimentary helmet is provided at no additional cost. Customers can only rent 2 helmets per booking. A Replacement fee will be imposed if the helmet is damaged or lost.
GPS Holder In case the GPS holder is missing or broken/damaged, replacement cost will be levied on the renter.
Petrol Batmobilz Rentals does not include fuel in the rental tariff. Batmobilz provides one litre fuel while delivery of vehicle and expects the rider to return the same amount of fuel while returning the vehicle. In case there is excess fuel in the vehicle at the time of return, we are not liable for any refunds for the same. In case there is shortage of fuel in the vehicle, the fuel charges as applicable have to be borne by the renter.
Misconduct Any misconduct with the station executive by the rider under the influence of alcohol or otherwise will result in cancellation of booking. The rider will not be liable to any refunds in such case.
Unauthorized Modifications or Repairs The Renter is not permitted to make any modifications or repairs to the Motorcycle. Any unauthorized changes will result in a penalty of ₹5,000 plus the cost to restore the vehicle to its original condition.
Unauthorized Rider Only the Renter specified in the rental agreement is permitted to drive the Motorcycle. A penalty of ₹2,500 will be charged if anyone other than the authorized Renter is found driving the vehicle.
Loss of Vehicle Documents The Renter is responsible for the safekeeping of the Motorcycle's original documents. In case of loss, a penalty of ₹3,000 will be charged for the issuance of duplicate documents.
Excessive Dirt or Soiling A cleaning fee of up to ₹500 will be charged if the vehicle is returned in an excessively dirty or soiled condition that requires special cleaning.

Payment of Penalties and Security Deposit

The security deposit paid by the Renter will be used to cover any applicable charges for damages, late returns, traffic fines, or other penalties listed in this policy.

  • If the total penalty amount is less than the security deposit, the remaining balance will be refunded to the Renter within 7-10 working days.
  • If the total penalty amount exceeds the security deposit, the Renter is liable to pay the difference immediately. The Company reserves the right to take legal action to recover the outstanding amount.
